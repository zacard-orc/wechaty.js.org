If you understand English, this section is for you!#

As the pace of society keeps getting faster, every day, we have more and more WeChat messages that need to be answered, and there are many repetitive questions that repeatedly find me. As a programmer, you are unwilling to waste your precious time in repetitive work.

Here, I will share with you how to use Wechaty and OpenAI to create a personal assistant that belongs to you. Help you deal with those repetitive problems and free up your time.

What are we going to do?#

A WeChat assistant robot has the following functions:

-I @ä»–, and when I ask questions, I automatically send out all the prepared materials -When others ask him, it can automatically match common questions and answer them -When no answer is matched, transfer it to me -The message is in the group and I am in the group: @æˆ‘ let me answer -Private chat or I am not in the group: send out my business card and guide to add me as a friend
